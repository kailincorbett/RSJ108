How to start a personal journal!

Assembly time: 1 hour
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Interested in starting a personal journal? It is fun and easy! Follow these steps to get started!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Journal of your choice
  • Pen
  • Glue
  • Scissors
  • Deocrations
  • Patience

  1. Find a journal to write in!

    The book can be simple or ornate. If you're happy with a simple journal,then buy a basic college-ruled notebook. If you want something more serious, look for a nice leather-bound journal. Anything works!

  2. Decorate your journal!

    Make the journal your own by infusing it with a unique style. Personalise the cover with words, art, stickers, colors. Or use magaize clippings to make a collage! If decorating isn't your thing feel free to leave it plain!

    Examples of decorated and unedecorated journals to gain insipiration!

  3. Think before you write your first entry!

    If you are having trouble finding your flow, self reflect before you write! Here are some ideas to write in your journal: write about what happened today, write about your feelings from today, write about what you learned or a "Learning Log", or turn your experiences into art!

    If you are a visual learner, take a peak at this YouTube video for aesthetic directions!

  4. Date your entry!

    If you are keeping a regular journal, its great to start dating your entry to establish a way to track when and what you wrote!

  5. Time yourself!

    While it is great to set aside time to journal, it may help to time yourself and let yourself go! The deadline may help you write efficiently!

  6. Write your first entry!

    Its time to get writing! The most important step of starting a journal is your first entry! All of the steps before are ways to make your journal comfortable for you to write! Now, write what is on your mind!
